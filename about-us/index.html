Our Story

About University Shoe Repair

Family-owned and operated in Tamarac, FL since 1990. Over 35 years of old world craftsmanship serving South Florida.

Who We Are

Craftsmanship Built on 35 Years of Trust

University Shoe Repair was founded in 1990 by Gary, a skilled cobbler who trained in traditional shoemaking before opening his shop on North University Drive in Tamarac. What started as a one-man neighborhood repair shop has grown into South Florida's most trusted destination for shoe repair, leather restoration, and clothing alterations.

We are a family-owned and operated business. That means every customer gets treated like a neighbor, every repair gets done right, and you always deal with someone who actually cares about the result. No corporate call centers, no upselling, no surprises on the price.

Our team brings together traditional cobbling skills and a professional seamstress on site to cover everything from resoling a worn-out work boot to restoring a Chanel handbag to showroom condition. We work on pieces from Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Prada, and all major luxury brands, as well as everyday footwear and clothing that matters to you.

Every job that leaves our shop carries a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If it is not right, we make it right.

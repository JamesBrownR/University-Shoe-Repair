What We Do

Shoe Repair & More in Tamarac, FL

From a broken heel to a full leather restoration, we handle it all in-house with the same attention to detail on every piece.

👟

Shoe Repair & Restoration

Sole replacement, heel repair, reconditioning, cleaning, dyeing, and more. We work on all shoe types from everyday sneakers to luxury designer footwear.

👜

Handbag & Leather Repair

Restore designer handbags, belts, wallets, and luggage back to their original condition. We work with Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Chanel, and all major luxury brands.

✂️

Alterations & Tailoring

Professional seamstress on site. Dresses, gowns, bridal wear, suits, pants, and shirts. Walk-in alterations welcome, no appointment needed.

👢

Western Boot Repair

Full western boot rebuilds, sole and heel replacement, leather conditioning, and restorations for all styles of cowboy and work boots.

🔧

Zippers, Belts & Hardware

Zipper replacement on shoes, bags, jackets, and luggage. Custom belt and wallet fabrication. Belt hole punching and hardware replacement.

Specialty Services

Orthopedic shoe modifications, dance shoe resoling, chair upholstery, scissor and knife sharpening, and patch additions to garments.

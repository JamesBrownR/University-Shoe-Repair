Belt Repair & Fabrication

Expert Belt Repair in Tamarac, FL

Whether your belt has worn out, torn, or simply does not fit the way it used to, University Shoe Repair can fix it or build you a new one. We have been handling belt repairs and custom leather fabrication in Tamarac since 1990.

Our craftsmen work with leather, suede, exotic skins, and synthetic materials. We can replace buckles and hardware, punch new holes, restitch failing seams, and refinish worn leather to bring a belt back to life. If you need something made from scratch, we do that too.

What We Do for Belts

  • Belt hole punching (add holes to any belt)
  • Buckle and hardware replacement
  • Belt strap shortening or lengthening
  • Leather conditioning and refinishing
  • Stitching and seam repair
  • Custom belt fabrication from scratch
  • Wallet strap and strap hardware repair
  • Bag strap and shoulder strap repair

Walk-ins are welcome Monday through Saturday. Bring your belt in, we will take a look and give you a free estimate on the spot. Most simple repairs like hole punching can be done same day while you wait.
